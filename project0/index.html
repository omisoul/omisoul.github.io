Basketball & Me

My journey in basketball started in grade 2, while attending Belair Prep. At first I found it to be really challenging, especially trying to score on normal size rims at the age of 7. I don't think I expected that I'd be doing it as long as I have.

I stopped playing basketball for three years due to switching schools, but after starting highschool I joined their basketball program. I spent seven years playing for Manchester High. In those seven years, I made many friends some of which are like my family now.

I'm really grateful for basketball and all it has done for me.

And as my coach would always say:

  • Offense Wins Games
  • Denfense Wins Championships
  • Free Throws Seal the Deal
